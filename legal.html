Affiliate disclosure

Myse is free. Some links to grocery stores and prep gear are affiliate links: if you buy through one, Myse may earn a small commission, at no extra cost to you. It never changes your price, and it never changes which recipes or products we show you — we only link the stores and gear a meal-prep kitchen genuinely uses.

As an Amazon Associate, I earn from qualifying purchases.

Myse also participates in the Walmart affiliate program (through the Impact network) and the Instacart Developer Platform affiliate program. When you tap a store button, those companies may set a cookie to credit a resulting purchase to Myse.

What Myse stores about you

Myse is a static website with no server of its own for your recipe activity. Almost everything you do stays in your own browser, in local storage on your device:

  • your favorites, ratings, and reviews;
  • your weekly plan and its shopping list;
  • your standing allergies;
  • your nutrition profile (the height, weight, age, sex, and goal used to estimate a calorie target) and your log of weight, lifts, and runs;
  • your display name and whether Myse Plus is active on this device.

This information is not sent to Myse, and clearing your browser's site data removes it.

When data does leave your device

  • Signing in. Myse offers an optional account. If email or social (Google/Apple) sign-in is enabled, it is handled by a third-party authentication provider, Supabase, which processes your email address to sign you in. If you never sign in, this doesn't apply.
  • Building an Instacart cart. If you use “Build my Instacart cart,” the ingredient names from your shopping list are sent to Instacart (through a small proxy service) so it can create the cart. Nothing else about you is sent.
  • Tapping a store or gear link. That takes you to Amazon, Walmart, or Instacart, where their own privacy policies apply.
  • Fonts. Pages load typefaces from Google Fonts, so Google may receive your device's IP address as part of serving them.

Myse does not serve third-party advertising networks, and it does not sell your personal information.

Children

Myse isn't directed to children, and its calorie-target tool deliberately refuses to compute a target for anyone under 18.

Your choices

You can use the whole site without an account, skip the store links entirely, and clear your saved data at any time through your browser's settings. Blocking cookies or third-party requests will still leave the recipes fully usable.

Changes & contact

If this policy changes, the date at the top will change with it. Questions about privacy or these disclosures can go to the project's public repository at github.com/Big-Sweat/meal-prep.
